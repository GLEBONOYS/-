The term "Negro" historically denotes a representative of the Negroid race, as well as the indigenous population of Tropical and South Africa. However, the word carries a negative connotation, since it is associated with the period of slavery, and in modern Western society is considered offensive and racist, especially in the context of the United States, where the terms "African American" or "black" are used.
